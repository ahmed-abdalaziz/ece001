In computer science, artificial intelligence (AI), sometimes called machine intelligence, is intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals. Among the most important of these characteristics 1- The ability to learn and infer 2- Reaction to situations not programmed in the machine
